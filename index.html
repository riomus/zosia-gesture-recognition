Motion detection: Optcal Flow Horn-Schunck

  • Works on BW images

  • Assumptions

    1. Pixel color (brightnes) do not change during movement

    2. Movement of neigbour pixels is similar

Motion detection: Optcal Flow Horn-Schunck

I(x+u,y+v,t+1)=I(x,y,t)

Motion discretization

Motion from optical from is returned for each pixel, steps that are taken to discretize whole image motion:

  1. Calculate average motion of whole image
  2. Calculate angle of motion vector
  3. Discretize angle using 'compass rose'
